Winners:

Electronics & Telecommunication

Sanskar Jain

Electronics & Telecommunication

Batch: 2021-2025

Our talented students conquered the challenging EcoMender Bot theme by developing a sophisticated robotic system from scratch using FPGA technology. The project involved creating a custom CPU architecture with Verilog HDL, implementing advanced sensor and actuator control systems, and establishing wireless communication with the central hub for autonomous arena navigation and resource collection.
