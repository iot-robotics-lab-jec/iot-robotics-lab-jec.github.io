Introduction

The IoT & Robotics Lab at the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, JEC, is a state-of-the-art facility established under the e-Yantra Lab Setup Initiative (eLSI) of IIT Bombay. Designed to bridge the gap between theory & practice, the lab provides students with hands-on exposure to microcontrollers, FPGAs, sensors, actuators, embedded systems, IoT, & robotics.

With a vision of fostering innovation, research, & skill development, the lab serves as a platform for students to explore cutting-edge technologies & develop solutions for real-world challenges. In addition to practical training, it also hosts MOOCs & workshops from IIT Bombay, enabling learners to stay aligned with the latest advancements in automation & smart systems.

The IoT & Robotics Lab stands as a milestone for JEC—empowering students to shape the future of technology with creativity, knowledge, & practical expertise.

Impact

Since its inception, the lab has already made a strong impact across multiple areas. Its members have successfully participated in the e-Yantra Robotics Competition, with several achieving commendable results. Others have secured admissions to top graduate programs and earned prestigious internships at IITs, IISERs, and other national institutes, demonstrating the effectiveness of the skills cultivated within the lab.

The lab has also supported the development of promising student-led projects, some of which have already been completed with success, while others are in progress & aimed at addressing real-world technological challenges. With a commitment to promoting research, collaboration, & hands-on innovation, the IoT & Robotics Lab continues to create opportunities for students to contribute to the future of technology & automation.
